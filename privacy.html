Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Quickshares.online, your privacy is our top priority. We are designed as an ephemeral, anonymous file-sharing service. We do not require registration, login, or personal information to use our basic services.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect what is strictly necessary to provide the service:

  • Uploaded Files: The files you upload are stored temporarily on our servers.
  • File Metadata: We store the file name, size, and upload time to manage the expiration process.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to track anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of visitors, country of origin) to improve our site performance.

2. How We Handle Your Files

This is a temporary storage service.

  • Encryption: Files are transferred via SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Auto-Deletion: All files are automatically permanently deleted from our servers 5 minutes after upload. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.
  • Access Control: Files are only accessible via the unique combination of the File Name, PIN, and OTP generated at the time of upload.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for the website's operation and for Google Analytics.

  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase: For database and file storage (Backend).
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous site traffic analysis.

5. Disclaimer

While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. Please do not upload files containing highly sensitive personal data (e.g., credit card numbers, passwords, government IDs).

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Email- contact.weebstudio@gmail.com.
